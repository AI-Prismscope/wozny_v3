# Your Intelligent, Local-First Data Workshop

Wozny combines the speed of local processing with the power of AI—without your sensitive data ever leaving your browser.

> **🛡️ 100% Private (Local Execution)**
>
> Zero-Knowledge Architecture. Your data never leaves your device.
> *   **Offline Capable:** Works without internet after initial load.
> *   **Regulatory Ready:** GDPR Compliant (Local Storage) & CCPA Ready.

## The Three Layers of Intelligence

### 1. The Analyst 📉
Instantly scans your CSV for missing values, duplicates, and formatting issues. Features **Type-Aware Smart Sorting** (Currency/Dates) and **Disciplined Smart Split** (Address/Name) with categorical filtering.

### 2. The Brain 🧠
Uses client-side Machine Learning to find semantic patterns, grouping variations like "Google" and "Google Inc" automatically using `transformers.js`.

### 3. The Assistant 💬
Talk to your data naturally. "Ask Wozny" uses an embedded LLM (`@mlc-ai/web-llm`) to filter, search, and reason about your information.

## How It Works

1.  **Upload** 📂
    Drag & Drop your CSV.
2.  **Review Insights** 🔍
    Health Score & Smart Analysis.
3.  **Ask Wozny** 🪄
    NLP Filtering & Search.
4.  **Fix in Workshop** 🔧
    Clean, Format, Smart Split, & Edit.
5.  **Export** 💾
    Download Clean Data.
